Pasta

Preparation and ingredients

Simple and quick to make, pasta is one of the most popular and essential store cupboard staples.

This palte is very fast to cook

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Oil

Recipe instructions

  1. Fill a saucepan with water, put the lid on and bring to the boil over a high heat.
  2. Add a good pinch of sea salt.
  3. Once the water is boiling, stir in the pasta.
  4. Cook the pasta according to the packet instructions, Its ready when its soft enough to eat
  5. Drain the pasta in a colander over the sink

Finaly, enjoy your pasta with Ketchup and some vegetables and cheese or tuna
