Rice

Preparation and ingredients

Rice is a very common plate at home, so lets enlist ingredients and how to cook it

With this plate you can accompany almos any other food

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Rice
  • 2 Garlic
  • 1 medium size Onion
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Oil

Recipe instructions

  1. Put the oil in a pot until get hot enought
  2. Fry garlic and onion, cut in slide
  3. Add 2 water cup until boil
  4. Add rice
  5. Lets cook until water dont cover the rice
  6. Set little fire
  7. Cover the pot and let cook for 20 minutes

Now lets enjoy your rice with fry egg and platano
