Soup

Preparation and ingredients

This homemade vegetable soup is healthy, easy to make, and tastes fantastic.

This vegetable-packed soup is easy to make and tastes incredible.

Ingredients

  • Sweat onions
  • Carrots
  • potatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Tomato paste
  • Garlic
  • Spices, pepper and salt

Recipe instructions

  1. Pour in vegetable or chicken stock, a can of diced tomatoes, potatoes, cabbage, and bay leaves.
  2. Simmer until the veggies are tender (about 20 minutes).
  3. Add frozen peas and cook for five more minutes.
  4. Season to taste with more salt, pepper, and a little acid like apple cider vinegar or lemon juice.

Enjoy your soup during a cool evening or maybe for brunch
